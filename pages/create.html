Eco-friendly
Neoprene Design

Soombi St.

A sustainable fashion project
inspired by Jeju’s haenyo divers,
Soombi Street repurposes Neoprene
scraps from wetsuit manufacturers
into handcrafted wallets and
bags. Through perseverance and
repeated outreach, the initiative
transformed an idea into reality,
highlighting the value of commitment
and daily effort in achieving
long-term goals.
